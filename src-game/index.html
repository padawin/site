Ghislain Rodrigues - Interactive website

Welcome. My name is Ghislain Rodrigues, programmer based in Berlin. I love to code, it is my job, but a hobby too.

When I code, I enjoy facing challenges, understanding algorithms, making things do stuff. I am very interested in game programming and learn through different personal projects.

This is my interactive portfolio. Once you click on the start button, you will be able to move your character around (using either your mouse, or the direction arrows on your keyboard).

Go grab my CV, my camera, or the Octocat. You can then use them from your inventory to access a PDF version of my CV, my travel pictures and my github profile.

You can also see a selection of personal projects I realised.

You will also be able to have a look at some more information about me such as my experience as a quest journal or my skills represented as the character's stats.

Please enjoy your visit and feel free to drop me a message!
