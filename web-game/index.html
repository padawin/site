The Programmer of Albion

Welcome. My name is Ghislain Rodrigues, programmer based in London. I love to code, it is my job, but a hobby too.

When I code, I enjoy facing challenges, understanding algorithms, making things do stuff. I am very interested in game programming and try to spend as much time as possible to learn different concepts to eventually see my own game be created.

This is my website, realised in the shape of a game. Once you will click on the start button, you will be able to move your character around (using either your mouse, or the direction arrows on your keyboard), go grab my CV, have a look at it in your inventory.

You will also be able to have a look at some more information about me such as my experience as a quest journal or my skills represented as the character's stats.

Please enjoy your visit and feel free to drop me a message!
